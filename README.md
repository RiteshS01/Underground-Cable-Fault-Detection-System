# ⚡ Underground Cable Fault Detection System

### 🔍 Smart Detection for Hidden Electrical Faults

Underground power cables play a crucial role in modern electrical distribution systems due to their enhanced safety, reliability, and protection from environmental conditions. However, when a fault occurs beneath the ground, locating the exact fault position becomes a challenging, time-consuming, and expensive task. Traditional fault detection methods often require extensive excavation, specialized equipment, and skilled manpower.

The **Underground Cable Fault Detection System** is an Arduino-based intelligent solution designed to identify and locate faults in underground power cables quickly and accurately. By utilizing the principle of **Ohm’s Law** and voltage-drop analysis, the system calculates the distance of the fault from the base station and displays the result in real time on an LCD screen.

This project provides a low-cost, portable, and efficient alternative to conventional fault-finding techniques. It reduces maintenance time, minimizes unnecessary digging, and helps utility providers restore power faster. The system simulates underground cable behavior using a resistor network and employs current sensing circuits, relays, and embedded programming to detect faults and determine their location with high accuracy.

## 🌍 Why This Project Matters

According to reports referenced in this project, underground cable failures are frequently caused by construction activities, insulation breakdown, weather conditions, and short circuits. Locating these faults manually often results in long power outages and increased repair costs. This system helps engineers and maintenance teams quickly identify the fault location, improving operational efficiency and reducing downtime.
